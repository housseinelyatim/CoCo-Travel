Le Royale Hammamet

Hammamet - Tunisia

-night stay
Your total is 167TND

This 5-star hotel is located in Tunisia’s tourist area. Le Royal Med Spa features an indoor pool, fitness center and beauty salon. Air-conditioned guest rooms offer a private terrace.

All elegant guest rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a minibar and extra long beds. Some suites feature a sitting area and kitchenette.

Seafood dishes and cocktails are served in Dana Club bar at Le Royal Hammamet. Italian cuisine is served at restaurant Capri and a buffet breakfast is provided every morning.

The spa at hotel Royal Hammamet features a sauna and provides beauty treatments including the 4-hand Ylang-Ylang massage.

An airport shuttle is available to Enfidha Hammamet Airport, 54.7 km away. Yasmine Golf Course is 6.4 km away.
